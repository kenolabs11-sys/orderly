# Code of Conduct

This project aims to maintain a respectful and constructive environment.

All participants are expected to communicate respectfully and provide constructive feedback.

---

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on improving the project
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences

---

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behavior is not tolerated:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Offensive language
- Spam or trolling

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or restrict participation if behavior violates these guidelines.

The goal is to keep discussions constructive and productive for everyone.
